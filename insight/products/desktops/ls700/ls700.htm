LS700

The Apricot LS700 combines the highest levels of performance in a mini-tower chassis that provides massive room for expansion. Because it comes with only the components you need, there’s no cost burden of unwanted frills and features with the LS700. The system will expand as your needs change; CD-ROM, tape streamer, additional hard drives, modem — almost without limit.
