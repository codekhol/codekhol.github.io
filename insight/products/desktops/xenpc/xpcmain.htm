XEN-PC (Samurai, A1, Revenge, Jade, P2 & Ruby Motherboards)

Initially launched as an entry level system, the XEN-PC first used a version of the XEN-LS II (Samurai) motherboard. Later versions use the A1 (486), Revenge, Jade and Ruby (Pentium) and P2 (486). The XEN-PCM version includes a CD-ROM drive & Sound Blaster card with internal loudspeakers. PCLS models include the Feature Card.
