LS Pro (Bonsai / Caracal Motherboard)

The natural progression and more powerful version of the LANstation, with increased speed, memory and hard disk capacity. The system is without an expansion bus, but includes 32-bit Ethernet and Enhanced Business Audio, as well as PCMCIA.


Model pictured above is Caracal.
