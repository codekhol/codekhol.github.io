LS500

The Apricot LS500 meets the needs of the most demanding business user. Fully specified, with a choice of Pentium® MMX, Pentium® II or Celeron processors, Ultra ATA hard disks and advanced SDRAM memory technology, these systems have been designed to provide the widest possible choice of price and specification, offering a choice of the Intel 440LX and 440BX chipset technologies for the Pentium II models and including a highly integrated hardware management controller.
