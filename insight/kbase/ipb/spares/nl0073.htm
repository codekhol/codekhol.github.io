MS600 ‘Promotional’ models

Three specific MS600 UK ‘promotional’ models shipped recently have caused some confusion as to which ‘spares’ part number Motherboards & Hard Disks should (& could) be used to service these units. The below information gives, by model, the spares part numbers for the Motherboard & Hard Disk shipped within each & also any replacement alternatives & the ‘Form, Fit & Function’ (FFF) differences :-

MY87230-1 - MS600 5200MMX:UK/32/4G/W95
Motherboard shipped :- Specification
M/B-Mustang/D J All – p/n 12449731 No WAVE facility
256K on-board CACHE
Motherboard replacement alternatives :- FFF differences
M/B-Mustang/D J Mic – PT12452731 None
M/B-Mustang/D HIGH END – PT12447031 Includes WAVE facility
512K on-board CACHE
Hard Disk shipped :- Specification
HD-4335MB Cyclone NoFerr – p/n 16255531 5.25" FORMAT
Hard Disk replacement alternatives :- FFF differences
HD-4320MB SE4320A – PT16136131 * 3.5" FORMAT
* Note :- Also requires a 3.5" HD MOUNTING PLATE PT15712131 for fitting.
  
MV87231-1 - MS600 5200MMX:UK/32/4G/W95
Motherboard shipped :- Specification
M/B-LIGHT/C J CONS 87307 Mic – 12455831** No WAVE facility
PAL video BIOS installed
Micron FLASH ROM
or
M/B-LIGHT/C J 87307 All – p/n 12460631** No WAVE facility
PAL video BIOS installed
Alliance FLASH ROM
** Note :- Spares / Hardware Bulletin No NL0072 (dated 03 April 1998) details an issue relating to ‘spares’ motherboards purchased as replacements under PT12455831 whereby these boards have an NTSC video BIOS installed & therefore will not output the display to a standard PAL television without a FLASH BIOS update being carried out prior to installation. This same issue also applies to PT12460631.
As the bulletin also suggests, an option for replacement ‘in the field’ of either shipped variant is to use either of the following higher specification alternatives that both have a PAL video BIOS installed as standard :-
Motherboard replacement alternatives :- FFF differences
M/B-LIGHT/C CONS 87307 Mic – PT12455631 Includes WAVE facility
M/B-LIGHT/C CONS 87307 All – PT12460431 Includes WAVE facility
Hard Disk shipped :- Specification
HD-4335MB Cyclone NoFerr – p/n 16255531 5.25" FORMAT
Hard Disk replacement alternatives :- FFF differences
HD-4320MB SE4320A – PT16136131 * 3.5" FORMAT
* Note :- Also requires a 3.5" HD MOUNTING PLATE PT15712131 for fitting.
  
MV85702-8 - MS600 6266:32/6.5G/W95
Motherboard shipped :- Specification
M/B-LIGHT/C CONS 87307 Mic – p/n 12455631 Includes WAVE facility
Micron FLASH ROM

* Note :- Also requires a 3.5" HD MOUNTING PLATE PT15712131 for fitting.
