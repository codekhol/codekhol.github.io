pintel x86

welcome to my `pinned intel x86 processor collection`. that's where the name comes from and it spans from the original 8086 released in 1978 to pentium 4 introduced in 2000. later pentium 4 models had no pins, and hence they are out of range of this collection (and also out of my interest). i have a few pinless pentium 4s though just to keep the generation.

most of my processors are new and never installed, some others are used but they are in excellent condition after these many years.
