    DIVA 2.0 POTS Option

    The POTS option is a complimentary product to the Diva 2.0 ISDN interface card when using Windows 95 ONLY. This device allows the connection of a regular telephone, fax machine or analog modem for operation over an ISDN line.

    The DIVA POTS Option is extremely handy for users who have access to ISDN but don't have separate analog lines – or want to avoid the expense of them. Simply add the DIVA POTS Option to your DIVA 2.0, plug in any analog devices, and you have an instant virtual office that needs only the ISDN line for all communications.
