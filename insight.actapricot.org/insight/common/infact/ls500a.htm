System Overview
The Apricot LS500 Series meets the needs of today's most demanding business users. Fully specified, with a choice of the very latest Pentium® and Pentium II® processors, Ultra ATA hard disks and advanced SDRAM memory technology, every LS500 is designed to offer the widest possible choice of price and specification, providing sufficient technology headroom to ensure it caters with the demands of software platforms both now and in
the future.

Using the latest Intel 430TX and 440LX chipset technologies and including a highly integrated hardware management controller the LS500 Series has been designed to provide the lowest ownership costs of any PC platform.

With 512KB Level 2 processor cache and 16-bit business audio as standard features on all models, LS500 systems are ready to exploit the power of new technologies such as Internet, voice messaging and other applications. Completing the specification is the optional x16 speed CD-ROM from market leaders, Sony.

Processor choice
The Pentium® Processor with MMX(TM) technology includes all of the features of a standard Pentium® processor, plus more. Because of its improvements you'll experience greater performance in all applications and smoother, more realistic results from multimedia based applications. With all major software vendors now supplying their standard software MMX enabled, MMX technology is a pre-requisite for system longevity. Pentium II® systems further enhance performance and with options up to 300MHz, applications previously available only on proprietary workstation platforms are now routinely being deployed on performance PCs.

Ready to network
Each LS500 system may be specified with an auto-sensing 10/100Mbit/s ethernet interface. These high performance 'plug & play' NICs provide optimum performance with current 10 Mbit/sec networks and 100Mbit/sec networks alike. Wake-on-LAN functionality, allowing remote management and support of client PCs simplifying IT support and reducing ownership costs. For clients with investment in other networking standards, LS500 systems can be supplied without a network card, allowing integration with Token Ring or ATM technology.

Easy to manage
All Apricot LS500 systems include enhanced manageability as standard. Designed to be compliant with the latest DMI 2 specification, each is supplied with a Windows application allowing a complete inventory of the system to be made ­ including firmware and software revision levels. Based upon established industry standards, the supplied application allows full co-operation with third-party network management tools; ensuring a simple and cost effective installation into both new and existing company sites.

In addition, each LS550 Pentium® system is supplied with 'Electronic Fingerprinting'. This allows your choice of information (e.g. company name, department, asset number, etc..) to be permanently recorded within each system, ensuring a permanent record of system ownership to be available. A sophisticated hardware management controller monitors the status of system critical items such as temperature, power and hard disk performance; controlling the fan speed to maintain an even system temperature; providing predictive drive failure notification and alerting the system administrator to

physical intrusion of the system case. This ensures that the LS500's reliability is maintained at maximum levels, further reducing cost of ownership.

Sounds good
All models incorporate integrated 16-bit audio allowing maximum use of today's multimedia rich operating systems and applications. Coupled with the x16 speed Sony CD-ROM, you can now have the benefit of high quality business audio through the unit's built-in speaker, or, for maximum quality through external speakers. Should your application demand additional multimedia capabilities, the on-board audio may be enhanced by use of the optional wave table module upgrade (or disabled completely allowing an alternate interface to be implemented).

Comprehensive range
Available with a range spanning entry-level to state of the art Pentium II® high performance models, the Apricot LS Series allows selection of the ideal model to meet your needs.

Connectivity
All LS Series products are available with multiple connectivity options. These ensure that the business advantages of Internet, Intranet and Extranet technologies can be fully exploited, allowing greater productivity and communications regardless of the size of your organisation. With integrated ethernet to high-speed modems and ISDN, all connectivity options have been investigated to give simple but effective connectivity to your local area network or even further afield to the World Wide Web, the electronic network that spans the globe.
