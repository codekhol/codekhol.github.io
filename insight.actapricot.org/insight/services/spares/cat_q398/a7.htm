Delivery

i)

Stocked items - Orders received prior to 2:30pm where stock is immediately available from the warehouse will be despatched via TNT, to your premises that evening for "Next Day" delivery free of charge on UK mainland. Should you require delivery at a pre-determined time such as "pre 9:00am", "pre-10:30am" or even "Sat am" then this can be processed at a premium and should be clearly marked on your order with acceptance of the delivery charge. Delivery to a different address to that of the purchasing company also incurs an extra delivery charge.

ii)

Non stocked items - Orders received for current parts that are not held in stock will be shipped within 5 working days from the date of the order (subject to availability). If an order is placed after 2:30pm or on a Friday, an extra working day will be required.

iii)

Discrete components - Orders received for discrete components that are not held in stock will be shipped within 30 working days from the date of order (subject to availability). If an order is placed after 2:30pm or on a Friday, an extra working day will be required.
